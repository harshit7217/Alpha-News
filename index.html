Israel Attacks

Currently, tensions between Israel and Iran are high due to a series of missile attacks. On October 1, 2024, Iran launched over 200 ballistic missiles targeting multiple Israeli sites, marking one of the largest direct assaults by Iran during the ongoing conflict. The strikes were part of Operation True Promise 2, which Iran described as retaliation for Israel's assassination of key leaders from Hamas and Hezbollah. While Israel intercepted most of the missiles, some hit areas near military bases and civilian locations, causing limited damage and minor injuries. Ayodhya Mandir

In recent news from Ayodhya, the district administration has implemented a ban on the sale of meat during the Navratri festival, which is being observed from October 3 to October 11, 2024. This decision was taken to respect religious sentiments during the nine-day festival dedicated to the worship of Goddess Durga

Additionally, preparations are in full swing for the grand 2024 Deepotsav celebrations in Ayodhya, scheduled from October 28 to 31. The event will be especially significant as it marks the first Deepotsav after the inauguration of the Ram Temple. Over 25 lakh diyas (lamps) will be lit, and there will be impressive light displays, cultural performances, and even a virtual reality platform allowing visitors to explore sacred sites
